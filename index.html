Our core product | The S1 Module.

  • Bluetooth 5.2 SoC
  • Industry lowest power FPGA
  • Li-cell charger
  • Buck-boost & LDO outputs
  • Remotely controlled low power & sleep
  • Flexible ADC inputs
  • SPI / I2C / I3C
  • Long range & Thread support
  • Integrated antenna, crystals and passives

Smaller than a MicroSD card, the S1 aims to simplify your next tiny battery powered product, without compromising on performance.

Application Demo | S1 ECG kit.

A convenient platform for developing ML algorithms for two, and three probe measurements. The S1 ECG kit is available as a low cost, easy to use battery powered board aimed at research, academia and teaching.

Initiatives in STEM and Space Education.

Building Stockholm’s first
open radio telescope.

Together with enthusiasts, and science community volunteers, we are helping to advanced STEM education initiatives in the heart of Stockholm City.

As lead system integrators, we're working closely with both space industry experts to build a robust platform, while also working closely with cultural and educational experts to ensure the device can become an effective open platform anyone can come and use in order to learn something new about space.

Come and visit us in Frihamnstorget to see the telescope in action, and join us in inspiring a new generation.
