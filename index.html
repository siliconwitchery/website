The tiny, yet powerful S1 module.

Always on machine learning for wearable and battery powered products.

  • Bluetooth 5.2
  • FPGA
  • Li-cell charger
  • Buck-boost & LDO output rails
  • ADC / SPI / I2C / I3C
  • Long range & thread support
  • Integrated antenna, crystals and passives

Smaller than a micro SD card, and designed for ultra low power. Always on inferencing can be achieved from as little as 75uA.

The S1 aims to simplify product design by combining best in class components into one easy to use module.
