Bluetooth 5.2 out of the box.
Based on the Nordic nRF52811, the S1 Module is future proof with support for Bluetooth direction finding, as well as Zigbee, Thread and ANT.


User FPGA fabric.
Fully configurable over the air. 8 DSP cores and 4 hardware serial drivers allow you to build applications that require a large number of sensors concurrently streaming data. The 5k of FPGA space is perfect for developing sensor aggregation logic, filtering and machine learnt models.


Complete battery management.
Charge and monitor all single cell Lithium technologies including Li-Ceramic. Adjustable buck-boost user voltage rails lets you dial in power management exactly for your application. Maximize battery life and reduce component count.


Tiny 6x12mm footprint.
With integrated antenna, flash memory, crystals and decoupling, your design footprint remains tiny. Just provide power and start building your application.


Get to market fast.
Fewer board revisions and a known working platform to let your software team start developing today.
