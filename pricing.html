S2 MODULE

Everything built in to help you go fast. The S2 Module with integrated antenna covers LTE bands 791MHz and up, ideal for mounting within plastic enclosures, or deployments within Europe.

• Global SoftSIM with data included
• Built in LTE-M antenna
• Built in GPS antenna
• Superstack OS pre-installed
• Lithium cell charging and monitoring
• GPIO incl. ADC, Serial, I2C & SPI

S2 MODULE EXT ANT

The S2 Module with external antenna connectors allows LTE coverage across worldwide bands. Ideal for mounting within sealed metal enclosures, or deployments within the US.

• Global SoftSIM with data included
• External U.FL connector for LTE-M
• External U.FL connector for GPS
• Superstack OS pre-installed
• Lithium cell charging and monitoring
• GPIO incl. ADC, Serial, I2C & SPI
