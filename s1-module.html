S1 system on module

Wireless AI compute in an ultra-small form factor. The brain for your next Bluetooth enabled wearable.

Block diagram

The S1 is a union of four key components critical to developing tiny battery-powered products. It targets applications requiring demanding algorithms while consuming as little energy as possible.

Video tutorials

We're creating lots of content to help get you started. Check out our YouTube channel for more deep dives and tutorials.

MicroPython or C SDK

For those needing to build algorithms and focus on data first, MicroPython is a perfect way to interact with your data and make changes on the fly. Connect wirelessly and access the S1 using our Web Bluetooth based REPL.

Alternatively, for those who need fine grained control, our minimal C SDK gives you a bare-bones configuration you can quickly start building on.
