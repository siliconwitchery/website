S1 system on module

Bluetooth – FPGA – Battery management

Unparalleled compute in an ultra-small form factor. All the RF, power and passive components are built right in. Simply add batteries, and the S1 is up and running.

Block diagram

The S1 is a union of four key components critical to developing tiny battery-powered products. It targets applications requiring demanding algorithms while consuming as little energy as possible.
