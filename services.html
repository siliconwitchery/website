Embedded hardware and systems engineers

Hardware

Experienced in HDI, RF and high speed layout. Our team is able to design production ready boards and fully assemble prototypes in house.

We specialize in ultra-small features for wearables and tiny edge IoT devices. Our designs often incorporate the leading-edge of integrated components including chip scale packages and 01005 passives.

Firmware

We ensure that firmware design starts from day one of the hardware design, to ensure all possibilities are covered.

Our multidliciplinary team are experienced in writing firmware for major ARM Cortex-M, Cortex-A and RISC-V devices. We are also able to provide software running on embedded linux and other popular RTOS platforms such as FreeRTOS, Zephyr and Azure RTOS.

Visit our Github

Control & Signal Processing

To really push hardware to the limit, we've adopted state of the art signal processing workflows. Using model based design techniques, we're able to develop realtime systems in a matter of days rather than months. By incorporating graphical math and simulation tools, we can develop extensive algorithms for RF, sensor fusion and machine learning on the edge.

Deployed code can also be configured according to various industry standard for safety and reliability such as MISRA. Our team can advise on such standards if required.

About Us

Silicon Witchery was founded in 2019 by Raj Nakarja, an ex-wireless systems engineer of Silicon Labs Inc. After years of experience developing devices for Wearables and IoT, he found many customers were struggling to integrate the advanced features of many of today's modern silicon devices into their products. With a unique background of both chip and product, he has formed a team of expert designers to help companies big and small bring their products to market.

Today, Silicon Witchery works with customers large and small across the globe, as well as develops it's own module based products for accelerating wearable and IoT products to market.
