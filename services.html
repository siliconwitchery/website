Hardware

Specialized in designing ultra-small circuits for wearbles and IoT, our team can help you with all of your HDI, RF and high speed layout.

We can assemble and test prototypes fully in house, as well as verify RF circuits and tune antennas.

With great access to certification partners, we can help advise on EMC testing and help to make sure you meet regulations for global market access.

Firmware

Our multidisciplinary team can help you build code for all major ARM, and RISC-V chipsets. We can also help with application development targeted to embedded Linux and RTOS based systems.

Pen Testing

Don't leave leave it to chance. We can check for a magnitude of vulnerabilities using the same tools as bad actors. We can also test for more sophisticated side channel attacks such as power or timing analysis.

About Us

Silicon Witchery was founded in 2019 by Raj Nakarja, an ex-wireless engineer of Silicon Labs Inc. and Bristol Robotics Lab. After many years of developing devices for Wearable and IoT customers, he started Silicon Witchery with a vision to help embedded designers integrate advanced devices and software into their products.

Today, the Silicon Witchery team works with customers large and small across the globe, and develops it's own module based products for accelerating wearable and IoT products to market.
